Here you will see a little from one of the greatest bands in Brazil.

As famous as big names like Vinicius de Moraes (samba) and Michel Teló (sertanejo), Charlie Brown Jr. is consagrated for playing alternative rock with a mix of genres reggae and rap.

Curious wikipédia fact:

"In 2015, the streaming platform Deezer reported that Charlie Brown Jr. was the second most-listened-to Brazilian band outside Brazil. In a Spotify survey also from 2015, the band placed 31st out of the 47 most-popular bands and artists."

We can surely see how big they are and still being and how famous and immersive for all kind of people, because their genius way to represent the life of people, doesnt mattering the age or society class.
